Loan_App

House Gryffindor 's Loan Calculator App can help you figure out what your monthly payment will be, and the total loan interest you will pay over the life of the loan. Simply enter your loan amount, interest rate and number of years in the form fields and click submit.

When shopping for any loan, it is a good idea to use a loan calculator. A loan calculator can help you narrow down your home or car search by showing you what you can pay each month. It can help you compare loan costs and see how differences in interest rates can affect your payments, especially with mortgages.

Our loan calculator app can give you an idea of the total cost of the loan expressed in annual percentage rate or APR. It definitely can answer a lot of questions and help you make smart financial decisions.

The colors for this app are picked from a house in Harry Potter called Gryffindor. The colors that represent Gryffindor are scarlet and gold, as these colors are associated with fire, or a fiery personality. The house is also represented by a ferocious lion with the following favorable traits: brave, chivalrous, noble, competitive, courageous, adventurous, willing to stand up for others.
